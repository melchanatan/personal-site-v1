Thing I've done

  • Feature Project

    Ursaminor Cansat

    A can-size satellite, installed with a camera and stabilizer capable of detecting and counting human, to then send the number of people back to the ground station. Aiming to aid in deep forest search and rescuse operation. Taking advantage of the fact that Cansat is launched into the sky by a rocket, which can theoretically be send further into the forest, without battery limitation that drone have.

    Python Tensorflow VisualStudio Fusion360

  • Feature Project

    Latthikorn-001 Rover

    Tasked with Designing a Rover model capable of carrying out excavation and/or In-Situ Resource Utilisation (ISRU) missions on the moon. We settled on utilizing lunar regolith's chemical makeup and processing it into building materials as a foundation of human lunar colonization. The challenge lies in the harsh interstellar environment and researching ways to overcome it.

    SolidWork SciHub

  • Feature Project

    Hydroponic System

    In this research base project, we focus on optimizing plant growth in hydroponic systems, with airflow being our point of interest. We designed a system centerized around mass flow tested by Computational Fluid Dynamic (CFD) simulation, built four identical systems, then collected data and compared the differences in growth and the optimal airflow it takes to grow microgreens.

    SciHub Arduino SimScale Fusion360
